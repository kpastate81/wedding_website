Location and Transportation

Location Information

The wedding and reception will be held at the Elk Country Visitor Center (Keystone Elk Country Alliance), Pennsylvania. The address is 950 Winslow Hill Road, Benezette, Pennsylvania 15821

Transportation

Elk County Visitors Center is an hour and half drive from State College, PA and a two-hour and thirty minute drive from Pittsburgh, PA. Ample parking is available at the Elk County Visitors Center for guests that are driving. Driving directions from the State College Airport to the Elk County Visitors Center are provided below.

Airport Information

The nearest airort is the State College/University Park Airport (SCE), located 1 hour and 22 minutes from the Elk Country Visitor Center
